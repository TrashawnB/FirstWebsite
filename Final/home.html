About Me

Hi, I’m Liana Makichyan! I’m from Nuremburg, Germany, and I am the founder of Liana's Bling Nails. I started this buisiness because I have a strong passion for designing nails and wanted to share my art with others. After many years of practice, work and support, I decided to take matters into my own hands and start my own business. Since I started Liana's Bling Nails in 2021, it’s quickly become one of the most popular nail salons in Columbia. I’ve been in your shoes before, so here at Liana's Bling Nails my goal is to provide you the best customer service and have you leaving here happier than you came .
